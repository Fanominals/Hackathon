Team Sprocket Hackathon

On the Hackathon Scouting App you can scout data from various teams in FIRST Robotics.


Analyze Page

On the Analyze Page you will find the statistics for the teams. The data on this page includes Starting Position, Habline Position, Highest Success, Highest Attempt as well as points scored during Autonomous period.

Ranking Page

On the Analyze Page, you can view the best teams in a certain category. The two categories include the Hatch and Cargo leaderboards. The hatch category corresponds to hatches scored, and the cargo corresponds to cargo scored.
